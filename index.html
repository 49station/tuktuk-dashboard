Phase A Focus — Konvy Beauty / Skincare, Lightweight Only

  • Lightweight = lower shipping cost = wider margin band
  • Sub-$30 beauty = impulse purchase decisions, faster sales velocity
  • Niche Thai brands (Srichand, Cathy Doll, Smooth E, etc.) compete well even against US-stock sellers
  • Curated catalog of ~50–300 SKUs beats bulk-list-everything approach (eBay rewards velocity, not volume)
  • Food + household categories deferred to a later phase
